Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 technology enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. Over the past 14 years, we've grown into one of the world's premier tech conferences, hosting over 10,000 attendees annually from more than 50 countries.

Our journey has been marked by groundbreaking announcements, including the debut of revolutionary technologies and thought-provoking discussions that have shaped the tech industry.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to bridge the gap between innovation and implementation. Our mission is threefold:

  • To showcase cutting-edge technological advancements
  • To foster meaningful connections between tech professionals
  • To inspire the next generation of innovators

We believe technology should be accessible, ethical, and transformative for all.
